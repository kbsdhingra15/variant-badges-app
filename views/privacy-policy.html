Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 10, 2026

Introduction

Variant Badges ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our Shopify app.

Information We Collect

Store Information

When you install Variant Badges, we collect and store:

  • Your Shopify store domain
  • Access tokens for API authentication
  • Product information (titles, variants, options)
  • Badge assignments you create
  • App settings and preferences
  • Subscription status and billing information (plan type, billing dates)

Customer Information

We do not collect, store, or process any customer personal data. Our app only works with product variant information and does not access customer names, emails, addresses, or any personally identifiable information.

Analytics Information

To help improve the app and understand badge effectiveness, we collect anonymous usage data:

  • Badge interaction events (clicks and views on product pages)
  • Product IDs and variant IDs where badges are displayed
  • Badge types used (HOT, NEW)
  • Timestamps of badge interactions
  • Anonymous session identifiers (no connection to customer identity)

Important: This data is completely anonymous and cannot be linked to individual customers. We only track which products and badges receive interactions, not who is interacting with them.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Variant Badges app functionality
  • Display badges on your product variants as configured
  • Save your badge assignments and settings
  • Authenticate API requests to your Shopify store

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in an encrypted PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway. We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure database connections
  • JWT-based authentication
  • Regular security updates

Data Retention

We retain your store data only while you have the app installed. When you uninstall Variant Badges:

  • All badge assignments are immediately deleted
  • All app settings are immediately deleted
  • All access tokens are immediately revoked and deleted

Third-Party Services

Variant Badges uses the following third-party services:

  • Shopify API: To access your product information and display badges
  • Railway: For secure hosting of our application and database

We do not share your data with any other third parties.

GDPR Compliance

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain rights under GDPR:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of data we have about your store
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your data (automatically done on uninstall)
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data

To exercise these rights, contact us at saffronapps15@gmail.com

Data Requests

As a Shopify merchant, you can request:

  • Data Export: We will provide your badge assignments and settings in JSON format
  • Data Deletion: Simply uninstall the app, or contact us for manual deletion

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
